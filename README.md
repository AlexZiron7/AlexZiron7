### 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a Senior Full-Stack Developer and entrepreneur based in Venezuela. I specialize in architecting, building, and deploying scalable digital solutions, multi-tenant SaaS platforms, and intelligent automation systems. As the founder of **Aurora Devs**, I help businesses transform complex requirements into clean, production-ready code with a heavy focus on SEO, structural efficiency, and high performance.

- 🛠️ **Current Ventures:** Leading product architecture and engineering at **Aurora Devs**.
- 🚀 **Recent Highlights:** - **Nerabook:** A multi-tenant SaaS ecosystem for appointment management in barbershops and spas.
  - **ZyncroPOS:** A robust Point-of-Sale (POS) system tailored for retail and hardware stores, integrated with local fiscal regulations (SENIAT).
  - **AutoRedVE:** A specialized management platform for mechanical workshops and advanced inventory control.
- ⚙️ **Automations:** Built serverless microservices and automated CI/CD workflows for real-time external data extraction (e.g., official currency tracking via GitHub Actions).
- 🧠 **Tech Philosophy:** I leverage advanced environments and AI-driven workflows to accelerate velocity, optimize architecture, and deliver pixel-perfect user experiences.
